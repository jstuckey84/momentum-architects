

## Remove Fake Testimonials & Trusted Organizations

**Why this matters:** Fabricated testimonials and fake company names on a live business site can violate consumer protection laws (e.g., FTC guidelines) and damage trust if discovered.

### Plan

**Option A — Remove the entire Testimonials section** (recommended if you don't have real testimonials yet):
- Remove the `<Testimonials />` component from `Index.tsx`
- Clean up the import

**Option B — Replace with a placeholder/CTA:**
- Keep the section but replace fake quotes with a call-to-action like *"Ready to transform your operations? Let's talk."*
- Remove the "Trusted by" logo strip entirely

### Recommendation

Go with **Option A** for now. Once you have real client testimonials and permission to use their names, we can re-add a polished testimonials section with authentic content.
